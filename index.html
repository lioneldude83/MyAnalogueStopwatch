Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 7, 2025

This app, MyAnalogueStopwatch, respects your privacy.

Data Collection

This app does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

  • No user data is transmitted to any servers.
  • No analytics or tracking tools are used.
  • No third-party services are integrated.
  • All stopwatch and timer data remains on your device.

Permissions

This app may request access to local device features (such as allowing live activities) strictly to support core functionality. These features do not involve data collection or tracking.

Contact

This app does not collect or process any personal data. No contact is required.
