Privacy Policy for MyChronoPro

Effective Date: September 18, 2025

MyChronoPro respects your privacy and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Data Collection

MyChronoPro does not collect any information from you. Specifically:

  • No user data is transmitted to any servers.
  • No analytics or tracking tools are used.
  • No third-party services or SDKs are integrated.
  • All stopwatch and timer data remains on your device.

Local Data

All stopwatch usage, lap records, settings, and session history in MyChronoPro are stored locally on your device. This information never leaves your device and is not accessible by the developer.

Permissions

MyChronoPro may request access to local device features (such as enabling Live Activities) strictly to support core functionality. These permissions do not involve any data collection or tracking.

Third-Party Sharing

No information is shared with third parties. No internet connection is required to use MyChronoPro.

Contact

This app does not collect or process any personal data. No contact is required.

This policy may be updated in the future. Any changes will be posted on this page.
