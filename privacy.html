Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 7, 2025

This app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Data Collection

We do not collect any data from you. There are no analytics, tracking, or third-party SDKs integrated into the app.

Local Data

All stopwatch usage, lap records, settings, and session history are stored locally on your device. This information never leaves your device and is not accessible by the developer.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not share any information with third parties. No internet connection is required to use the app.

Contact

This app does not collect or process any personal data. No contact is required.
